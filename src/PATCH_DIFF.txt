LAYAN GOLD v9 PATCH - CONCRETE DIFFS
================================================================================

VERDICT FIXES APPLIED:

1. STROKE-WIDTH (Fix sub-pixel aliasing)
   OLD: stroke-width="0.6"
   NEW: stroke-width="1"
   RATIONALE: 0.6 SVG units at 32px render = 0.38 actual pixels (sub-pixel,
             causes aliasing). 1.0 = 1 pixel (clean rendering).

2. SHADOW OFFSET (Fix asymmetric shadow)
   OLD: <feOffset dx="0.5" dy="0.8" ... />
   NEW: <feOffset dx="0" dy="0" ... />
   RATIONALE: Offset caused shadow to not align with outline on bottom-right,
             making top-left appear bare. Symmetric shadow fixes this.

3. SHADOW OPACITY (Enhancement)
   OLD: opacity="0.35"
   NEW: opacity="0.40"
   RATIONALE: Slightly more defined without over-darkening.

4. OUTLINE COLOR (No change needed)
   EXISTING: #FFF6E0 (cream)
   STATUS: Correct per industry standard (Bibata, Phinger use white/cream
          for gold outlines). Keep as-is.

DECISION TREE
================================================================================

Q: Keep the outline?
A: YES. The outline is correct. The problem was rendering (aliasing), not design.

Q: What if aliasing still visible after this patch?
A: Increase stroke-width to 1.2, or render at higher DPI and downsample.

Q: Size-specific stroke-width?
A: Not in this patch (uses 1.0 for all sizes). See PATCH_IMPLEMENTATION.md
   section "RENDER-SIZE-AWARE NOTES" if you need 32px→1.0, 64px→1.0, 128px→0.5.

Q: Remove shadow entirely?
A: No. Shadow is fine. Just make it symmetric (this patch does that).
